Karibu kwenye tovuti ambayo utajifunza kila kitu kuhusu Kenya

1.Kenya is located in East Africa, on the equator.

2. The population of Kenya is about 48.6 million.

3.The "Big Five" animals can all be found in Kenya. These animals are rhinos, leopards, lions, buffalo, and elephants.

4.The capital and largest city is Nairobi

5. The two official languages of Kenya are English and Swahili, but there are dozens of indigenous languages spoke throughout the country.

6. Coffee is a huge export in Kenya, but it is not consumed in the country. Kenyans believe that all of the coffee they produce should be sold outside of their country, so they drink tea or beer.

7. The country is named after Mt. Kenya, which is the highest point in the country and is 17,057 feet high.

8. Lions, leopards, elephants and buffalo can all be found running free in Kenya. Due to this, many tourists who visit the country go on safaris.

9.Kenya is widely known around the world for its distance runners. The country frequently has runners win Olympic medals for middle and distance running.
